Lucky Love

How lucky am I to meet her, so fair,
A chance encounter beyond compare,
In her smile, I find a world so bright,
Guiding me through each day and night.

Her laughter, like a soothing melody,
In her presence, my heart dances free,
How fortunate am I, destiny's hand,
Led me to her, where love does stand.

In her eyes, I see the stars align,
A love like hers, forever shall shine,
How fortunate am I, to be her one,
Together, under the moon and sun.

With her, life's journey feels so right,
How lucky am I, through day and night,
In her arms, I find my refuge and grace,
Forever in her love, I'll find my place.
